If we change the `angle` calculation slightly into:
```
const angle = i * PHI
```
We get the golden ratio sunflower spiral ✨✨✨
---
`PHI` is the golden ratio. 

By multiplying it with `i` every next dot gets drawn a `PHI` angle away.
---
